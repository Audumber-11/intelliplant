Our Solution

Six specialized AI agents working together to detect, predict, and prevent safety incidents before they occur.

Risk Engine

Multi-sensor correlation with 6 compound patterns. 20 configurable safety rules covering OISD-116, Factory Act, DGMS.

Geospatial Heatmap

Real-time IDW-interpolated risk visualization across 225 grid cells. WebSocket-driven updates under 500ms.

Incident Intelligence

Pattern detection, root cause analysis, and recurrence prediction. Similar incident matching for investigation support.

Permit Intelligence

Automated conflict detection between overlapping permits. Risk scoring with conditional safety requirements.

Emergency Orchestrator

ICS plan generation, auto-escalation, muster point tracking. Response time monitoring and metrics.

Compliance Audit

OISD-116 and Factory Act checklists. Automated compliance scoring with finding categorization.
